About

Benjamin Tung is a civil engineer from the University of Illinois at urbana Champaign and has been charting since 2016. He made his debut in Zenius-I-vanisher.com's Summer Contest 2016 and continued on to winning first place in Technical Showcase 2.

He likes charting EDM songs, specifically in genres like future bass, glitch hop, and electro. He plays the drums and likes dogs. Except Ian Klatzco's dog. Fuck that dog. Except don't actually fuck it. Where is this going?
